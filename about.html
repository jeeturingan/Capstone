About the System

Overview

    The 3D Braille System is empowered by Java. With the use of the system, users can create 3D Tactile Storyboard using the object templates and users can also add braille text by letting the system convert text to braille text. Users can also customizetheir own storyboards by adjusting different settings on the Manual tab. The system also has a converter that allows the conversion of 2D images to 3D object models. The user can upload an image and the system can convert it automatically.


Mission and Vision


    The 3D Tactile Storyboard wants to help the visually impaired, as well as their teachers and relatives, to give them learning materials based on what they specifically want. With the use of the system, anyone can teach anybody whatever they want.

    We serve to empower the visually impaired people of all ages to create personal confidence, positive attitude towards their illness, and to help them harnest their skills despite their illness.

    The 3D Tactile Storyboard aims to help the visually impaired to see a much larger scope of the world. The goal is to remove the limits of the old learning materials that the visually impaired are using and help them learn more about everything. We also provide the most innovative and useful methods in teaching Filipino to the visually impaired to further their knowledge about the language. We aim to create a world where the visually impaired almost has the equal capabilities with everyone in the world, despite their illness, and help them overcome the challenges brought by their illness.
